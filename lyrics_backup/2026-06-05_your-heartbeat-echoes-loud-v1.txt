[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
Your heartbeat echoes loud inside this quiet room
I feel the fire pulling closer in the gloom
One touch is all it takes to lose my sense of time
I want to cross the border, make you fully mine

[Verse 1]
The shadow dances right across the wooden floor
I know I've never wanted anyone this much before
I pull you closer, let the silence fade away
Just read the hidden words my eyes are trying to say

[Pre-Chorus]
The night is closing in, but we are shining bright
I feel the electricity when you hold tight
We leave the heavy world a million miles behind
I trace the lines of everything I hoped to find

[Chorus]
Your heartbeat echoes loud like thunder in the dark
A sudden spark that leaves a permanent mark
I breathe you in and let my wild defenses fall
I hear the rhythm and I answer to the call

Yeah, I hear it answering the call
